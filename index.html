Action Kamen is a fictional superhero character from the popular Japanese anime series "Crayon Shinchan". He is the favorite superhero of the show's protagonist, a mischievous and curious 5-year-old boy named Shinnosuke Nohara, who dreams of becoming a hero like him. Action Kamen wears a red suit with a yellow belt and has various gadgets, including a mask and a gun. The character is often featured in the show's storylines as Shinnosuke pretends to be him or imagines himself in situations where he gets to help people with his own superhero abilities.
